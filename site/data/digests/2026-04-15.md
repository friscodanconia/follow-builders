**Agent composability drives Cognition usage surge**
AI researcher Swyx reported that usage of Cognition's Devin coding agent has roughly doubled globally since the company launched agent composition features and proactive capabilities. Users are discovering creative applications when they can chain agents together and make them take initiative rather than just respond to prompts.
Why it matters: This is the first real data point showing that agent-to-agent interaction drives adoption more than individual agent quality. Your company's AI strategy might be focused on the wrong metrics.

**The productivity trap that's fooling AI power users**

He noted how easy it is to mistake setup optimization for real productivity because it feels like progress and is genuinely fun.
Why it matters: If you're spending more time tweaking your AI workflow than shipping actual projects, you've become a tool collector, not a builder. The most productive AI users are often the ones with the simplest setups.
