**AI Builders Digest — April 20, 2026**

Every company is about to become a software company whether they planned for it or not. The question isn't if your business will build AI agents — it's whether you'll be ready when automation becomes cheaper than hiring.

**Box CEO Aaron Levie says every company will become a software builder**
Levie argues we're thinking too small about where software engineering jobs will come from. Beyond tech companies building apps, AI agents are making it economically feasible for every bio lab, law firm, and manufacturing company to create custom automation software that would have been technically impossible or too expensive before. "Every other company on the planet starts to create software for bringing automation to their workflows."
Why it matters: If Levie's right, your local dentist office will soon have someone writing AI workflows. The demand for people who can build with AI tools is about to explode far beyond traditional tech jobs.
