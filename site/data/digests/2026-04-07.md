**Anthropic commits to keeping Claude ad-free**

Anthropic announced that Claude will remain advertisement-free, arguing that ad-based revenue models conflict with building genuinely helpful AI assistants. The company explained that advertising incentives would compromise user trust and detailed plans to expand access through other means. This puts Anthropic at odds with potential future business models from competitors.

Why it matters: This is a bet that people will pay directly for AI assistance rather than accept "free" AI that's optimized to show them ads — setting up a fundamental split in how AI companies make money.

OpenAI published a detailed industrial policy proposal for managing the transition to widespread AI, focusing on expanding economic opportunity and building resilient institutions. The document presents their vision for how governments should approach AI development, workforce transitions, and wealth distribution as AI capabilities advance. It's part policy manifesto, part lobbying document.

Why it matters: As AI companies gain more influence, they're not just building technology — they're actively trying to shape the laws and policies that will govern their industry.
