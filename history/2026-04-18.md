**Product manager Peter Yang builds AI agents that grade each other**
Yang described his current approach to AI workflows: he creates one agent to do the work, then spins up a separate "eval agent" to grade the first one's output with yes/no checks. If the evaluation isn't "yes" across the board, the grading agent tells the working agent to try again. He's currently building this setup for YouTube thumbnail and title creation.
Why it matters: This is what actually useful AI automation looks like in 2026. Not one perfect agent, but two imperfect ones checking each other's work. Your marketing team should steal this pattern.
