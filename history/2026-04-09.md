**Box CEO shows AI agents filling out RFPs in real time**
Box CEO Aaron Levie demonstrated the company's new AI agent automatically completing a request for proposal response using the company's existing knowledge base. The process, which normally takes hours of focused work, runs autonomously while employees handle other tasks. Levie calls this "the jump when you go from having a chatbot to being able to actually have an agent go off and do work for minutes or even hours."
Why it matters: RFP responses are where deals get won or lost, and they're currently a massive time sink for every B2B company. If AI can handle the grunt work of pulling together technical specs and case studies, sales teams can focus on strategy instead of copy-paste.
