**Claude moves into Microsoft Word — and that changes everything**
Anthropic launched Claude for Word in beta this week, letting users draft, edit, and revise documents directly from the sidebar. Claude preserves formatting and shows edits as tracked changes, just like a human colleague would. It's available on Team and Enterprise plans only.
Why it matters: This isn't about replacing copywriters. It's about making every knowledge worker 3x faster at the document grind that eats half their day. When AI lives inside Word instead of a separate tab, the friction to use it drops to zero.

**Anthropic's Thariq Shihipar on cloud vs local AI development**
Anthropic engineer Thariq Shihipar shared insight about the division between planning and implementation in AI coding: planning can happen in the cloud since it's mostly reading code and understanding intent, but implementation often requires local environments and interactivity.
Why it matters: Every startup building "AI that codes" is making this tradeoff. The companies that get the cloud-local split right will win the developer tool wars.
