**Hugging Face shows how to build culturally grounded Korean AI agents**
The company published a detailed guide on creating AI agents that understand real Korean demographics using synthetic personas. Why it matters: Most global AI companies are still building one-size-fits-all agents and wondering why they fail in local markets. This is a blueprint for agents that actually work in specific cultures, not just specific languages.

**Flatiron Health's Zara Zhang predicts the death of internal product meetings**
Zhang argues that as AI handles more implementation work, product teams should spend most of their time talking to external users rather than internal stakeholders. Her reasoning: figuring out what to build becomes infinitely more valuable than actually building it when AI can handle the construction.
Why it matters: Your weekly product planning meetings are about to become monthly strategy sessions. The teams that shift to customer research mode first will build better products while their competitors are still optimizing their internal processes.
