**Google launches specialized TPUs for the "agentic era"**
Google unveiled its eighth-generation TPU chips designed specifically for AI agents. The new TPU-8T and TPU-8I processors are optimized for the multi-step reasoning and tool use that define agent workflows, rather than simple text generation.
Why it matters: Google is betting that AI agents will need fundamentally different hardware than chatbots. If they're right, every company building agents on standard GPUs is about to hit a performance wall that only Google can solve.
